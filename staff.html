Our Qualified Teachers

At Goalagara Path Mandir, we understand that our teachers ultimately determine the success of our students. In keeping with its mission to provide a truly education at par the international standard, teachers having considerable previous teaching experiences will be recruited. All faculty members are graduates with professional teaching qualifications and previous experiences in the field of education. Specialists providing the very best in career and university guidance have also been appointed to help students get into world class colleges and universities after completing their education at Goalagara Path Mandir
